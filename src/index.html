(Unofficial) Butte County COVID-19 Dashboard

Data assembled by Christian Hammond based on the Butte County dashboard, California hospital data, and other data feeds.

County data is updated on weekdays by 4PM, and other data is updated as it becomes available (but is generally one day behind).

Learn more in the Frequently Asked Questions and follow the updates on Facebook and Twitter.
